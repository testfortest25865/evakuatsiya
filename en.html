The history of the origin of our company

Since January 2011, under the leadership of our director Agil Zeynalova, the company started its activity as an evacuation service in Azerbaijan under the logo "156".
Further, starting from 2012, under the logo "166 Cargo Transportation and Logistics" the company begins to carry out transportation with only a few trucks.

To date, the department of evacuation and cargo transportation is huge, and has several hundred cars.
Our tow trucks are deployed in all regions of the republic, which promotes operational service to clients 24/7.

Our transportation is not limited to the borders of the Republic of Azerbaijan. And services continue to expand...
